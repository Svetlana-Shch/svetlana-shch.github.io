GPU are considered one of the most important components of computing systems, providing resources for solving various problems. They are used in video games, neural networks, scientific activities, Big Data, cryptography and many other areas.

Controller is a programmable device that controls and monitors other devices or systems. They are used in a variety of areas, from automation of manufacturing processes and control of technological systems to regulation of home appliances and solar panels.

CPU is the main component of computing devices that processes data and manages all system tasks. It is the processor that performs all the calculations and logical operations that are needed for computer programs to work.
